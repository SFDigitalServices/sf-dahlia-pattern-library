Claiming a preference on your housing application is optional. Preferences qualify you for a higher ranking in the lottery.

All documents must show the correct name and current address. All documents other than a lease must show a date within 45 days of your application.

Live in Neighborhood, Live in San Francisco and Anti-Displacement Housing Preferences

You'll need 1 (one) of the showing with the address where you live in San Francisco:

  • Telephone bill (land line only)
  • Cable and internet bill
  • Electric bill
  • Gas bill
  • Water bill
  • Paystub with home address
  • Public benefits record
  • School record
  • Letter documenting homelessness

Work in San Francisco Preference

You'll need 1 (one) of the showing with the address where you work in San Francisco:

  • Paystub with employer address
  • Letter from employer

Assisted Housing Preference

You'll need a Copy of Lease showing the address where you live.

Rent Burdened Preference

You'll need a Copy of Lease showing the address where you live.

You'll also need to show last months rent with 1 (one) of the following:

  • Money order
  • Cancelled check
  • Debit from your bank account
  • Screenshot of online payment
